Applied Intuition – Website IA & GTM Evolution

From single-product seed to platform posture

2018 – Seed Stage: Object Sim (Single Product)

Product
  • Object Sim = scene engine for virtual driving scenarios.
  • Defines ground truth world: roads, actors, weather, lighting.
  • Teams could run millions of safe miles in sim instead of on-road.
  • Means: build scenarios like “child runs into road at dusk,” run 1,000x with variations, measure outcomes.
Validation / Why it mattered
  • AV programs asked “how many miles are enough?”
  • Simulation provided scalable scenario coverage, not just log replay.
  • Edge cases caught virtually before expensive road tests.
Website IA
  • Very simple: “Simulation software for AVs.”
  • Only About, Careers, Contact pages.
Focus & GTM
  • Positioned as “safety mileage multiplier.”
  • Sold bottom-up to AV labs (Cruise, Zoox, Argo).
  • Demos on specific pains (night pedestrians, unprotected turns).
  • Founder credibility (ex-Google/Stanford) helped close early pilots.

2019–2021 – Expanding to a Simulation Suite

New Product Modules
  • Sensor Sim: renders what sensors “see” from Object Sim ground truth.
    – Cameras: pixels after optics, motion blur, noise.
    – LiDAR: point clouds with range noise, weather attenuation.
    – Radar: detections with Doppler, clutter, multipath.
    Means: perception errors come from sensor physics, so models need realistic inputs.
  • Log Sim: replays timestamped raw sensor logs from real drives.
    – Test on true field distributions (sun glare, dirty lens).
    – Blend synthetic actors into real logs: e.g. add a cyclist at night.
    Means: anchors sim to real world & augments missing cases.
  • Cloud Engine: runs massive scenario sweeps in parallel.
    Means: turn test catalog into daily CI gate; no workstation bottlenecks.
Integration back to Object Sim
  • Object Sim = perfect world state.
  • Sensor Sim = projects that into sensor space with imperfections.
  • Log Sim = validates stack on real-world distributions.
  • Cloud = scales it all into coverage metrics.
Packaging & GTM
  • Website now had Products section: Object, Sensor, Log, Cloud.
  • Modular SKUs: land with one, expand later.
  • Cross-sell into OEM R&D teams; bundle pricing.
Validation
  • Case studies (Toyota, VW labs).
  • Papers citing Applied tools.
  • Reputation as default sim suite.

2022 – Vehicle Dynamics + HIL

Key Expansion
  • Acquired CarSim → high-fidelity vehicle dynamics models.
  • VehicleSim: tires, suspension, braking, steering physics.
  • HIL: connect real ECUs/controllers into virtual scenarios.
Why
  • Safety depends on vehicle response, not just perception.
  • Means: test stopping distances, stability, controller tuning before track.
Product Strategy & GTM
  • Extend from perception-only → full system validation.
  • Appeal to validation & safety teams (ISO 26262).
  • Larger ACVs; multi-year V&V contracts.
Validation
  • OEMs caught pre-track defects earlier.
  • Differentiated from niche perception-only sim vendors.
